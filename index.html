About
me

I have a Master degree in marine ecology and I am currently doing my PhD in evo-devo on elasmobranch fishes, especially on catsharks. I try to describe their tooth shape diversity using microCT scans and 3D geometric morphometrics. Then, I am deciphering what drives such diversity, including physical and molecular parameters.
